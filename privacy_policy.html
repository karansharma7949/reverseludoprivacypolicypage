1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve gameplay features (online matchmaking, friends, leaderboards), troubleshoot issues, and show ads (if enabled in your build).

  • Account information (if you sign in): email address, display name/username, and authentication identifiers.
  • Gameplay data: game state and events needed to run matches (e.g., room ID, player moves, dice results, winners, timestamps).
  • Device & app information: device model, operating system version, app version, language, and approximate region (derived from device settings).
  • Diagnostics: crash logs and performance data to help us fix bugs.
  • Advertising identifiers (if ads are shown): a device advertising ID (such as Google Advertising ID) and related ad interaction data.
Note: We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information. Please do not share sensitive information in chat or usernames.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide gameplay features such as online matches, friends, and leaderboards.
  • Maintain and improve the App, including debugging and performance tuning.
  • Prevent fraud and abuse (e.g., cheating, spam, or misuse).
  • Show ads and measure ad performance (if your version includes ads).

3. Sharing & Third Parties

We may share information with service providers who help operate the App. These providers process information on our behalf and only as needed to provide their services.

  • Backend & database services: we may use a backend provider (e.g., Supabase) to store user accounts, game rooms, and match results.
  • Crash reporting and diagnostics: providers may receive crash logs and basic device/app metadata.
  • Advertising networks: if enabled, ad partners may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data.

4. Advertising

The App may display ads using third-party advertising SDKs (for example, Google AdMob). These partners may use cookies or mobile identifiers to provide, personalize (where permitted), and measure ads.

You can reset or limit ad personalization from your device settings.

5. Analytics

We may use analytics to understand how players use the App (e.g., session duration, feature usage, errors). Analytics data is used to improve stability and gameplay.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, including:

  • Account data while your account is active.
  • Gameplay records as needed for leaderboards, dispute resolution, and anti-cheat.
  • Diagnostics data for a limited period to troubleshoot issues.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Choices & Rights

  • Access/Update: you may update certain profile information within the App where available.
  • Delete account: if your version of the App supports it, you can request account deletion. Otherwise, contact us and we will assist.
  • Opt out of personalized ads: via device-level ad settings.

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can delete it.

10. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own depending on where our service providers operate. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards where required.
